About 24 Dimensions

Pune’s Real Estate Mandate & Project Marketing Partner

24 Dimensions is a Pune-based real estate mandate and project marketing firm. We partner with developers, landowners and premium homebuyers to plan, position and sell residential and plotting projects across Pune’s key micro-markets.

Our Story

Born from the New Reality of Real Estate

With RERA, GST and changing buyer expectations, real estate in Pune has gone through a complete reset. Traditional, ad-hoc ways of selling – scattered campaigns, unaligned sales teams, no data – simply stopped working.

Some developers adapted quickly

  • Product mix and pricing didn’t match the micro-market
  • Marketing spoke one language, sales another
  • There was no single owner of the entire mandate – from research to handover
